\# Week 4 – Advanced Red Teaming



\## Overview



Week 4 focuses on \*\*advanced red team concepts and hands-on simulations\*\*, covering command-and-control (C2), cloud attacks, adversary emulation, evasion, automation, living-off-the-land techniques, and professional reporting. All activities are conducted \*\*strictly within isolated virtual lab environments\*\* for academic purposes.



\## Learning Objectives



\* Understand and simulate \*\*advanced C2 frameworks\*\* and stealthy communication

\* Perform \*\*cloud reconnaissance, privilege escalation, and data exfiltration\*\* in lab setups

\* Emulate real-world threat actors and test blue team detection

\* Apply \*\*native tool abuse\*\* to minimize detection

\* Produce \*\*PTES-aligned reports\*\* and executive briefings



\## Tools \& Platforms Used



\* Kali Linux, Windows 10 (Evaluation)

\* Cobalt Strike, Metasploit, Caldera

\* Pacu, awscli, CloudGoat

\* PowerShell, WMI

\* Google Docs, Draw.io
